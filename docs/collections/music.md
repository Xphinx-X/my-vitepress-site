# 🎵 Music

Streaming, downloads, and players.

## 🎧 Streaming
- **[Spotify](https://spotify.com)** - The giant of music streaming.
- **[YouTube Music](https://music.youtube.com)** - Great for mixes and live tracks.

## 🎶 Downloading
- **[Soulseek](https://www.slsknet.org/)** - Peer-to-peer file sharing for rare music.
- **[Deemix](https://deemix.app/)** - Download tracks from Deezer.

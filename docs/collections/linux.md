# 🐧 Linux

Distros, ricing, and open-source goodness.

::: tip Recommendation
Since you are on CachyOS Hyprland, you are already running one of the coolest setups! Here are some tools to make it even better.
:::

## 🐧 Distros
- **[CachyOS](https://cachyos.org/)** <Badge type="tip" text="Arch Based" /> - The distro you are using! High performance.
- **[Bazzite](https://bazzite.gg/)** - An OS for gaming and creators.
- **[NixOS](https://nixos.org/)** - Reproducible and declarative OS.

## 🎨 Ricing & Window Managers
- **[Hyprland](https://hyprland.org/)** <Badge type="tip" text="Wayland" /> - Your dynamic tiling WM.
- **[Waybar](https://github.com/Alexays/Waybar)** - Highly customizable bar for Wayland.
- **[Rofi Wayland](https://github.com/lbonn/rofi/wiki/wayland-support)** - App launcher.

## 🛠️ Utilities
- **[Kitty](https://sw.kovidgoyal.net/kitty/)** - The fast, GPU-based terminal.
- **[Fastfetch](https://github.com/fastfetch-cli/fastfetch)** - Neofetch alternative to show off your setup.
